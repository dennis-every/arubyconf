Aruba Ruby
Global summit
2023

Aruba Ruby Global Summit is an annual gathering for all who are passionate about Ruby and Ruby on Rails. The Aruba based conference has served the Ruby community since 2008.

2023.01.04(THU) ~ 05(FRI)

@Cas di Cultura, Oranjestad, Aruba

Main Program

  • Lecture

    Listen to speakers from various countries about how they've used Ruby on Rails to build applications.

  • RoR Exhibition

    Appreciate various creations using Ruby on Rails, organized by Microverse

  • Forum

    Have the time to share your thoughts and opinions with topic experts.

  • Workshop

    Create new applications leveragin the power of open source code, frameworks, and libraries.

  • RoR ignite

    Get opportunities to network with Ruby on Rails affiliates around the world, also after the summit.
